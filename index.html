Good questions

Before you buy

Is it a real detector or a toy?
Real. It has a backlit digital target-ID screen, discrimination to skip junk metal, and a waterproof coil that finds coins down to about 8 inches. It's a genuine entry-level hobby detector — just far easier to use than a pro machine.
Is it hard to use? I've never detected before.
No — that's the whole point. Turn it on, choose a preset mode, and sweep. There's no manual ground-balancing to fuss with, and the included field guide walks you through your first hunt step by step.
Can I use it at the beach or in water?
Yes. The search coil is waterproof, so you can hunt wet sand, the surf line, riverbanks and shallow water. (Keep the control box on the shaft out of the water.)
Is it good for kids or as a gift?
It's one of the most popular gifts in the hobby. The adjustable shaft fits kids and adults, it's lightweight, and it comes as a complete kit — ready to hunt out of the box.
What does shipping cost?
Shipping is completely free in the US, and so are returns. Most orders arrive within 3–6 business days.
