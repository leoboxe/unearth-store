Frequently Asked Questions

Is it a real detector or a toy?+

Real. It has a backlit digital target-ID screen, discrimination modes to skip junk metal, and a waterproof coil that finds coins down to about 8 inches. It's a genuine entry-level hobby detector — just far easier to use than a pro machine.

I've never detected before — is it hard?+

No. Turn it on, pick a preset mode, and sweep. There's no manual ground-balancing to fuss with, and the free field guide walks you through your first hunt step by step.

Can I use it at the beach or in water?+

Yes — the search coil is waterproof, so you can hunt wet sand, the surf line, riverbanks and shallow water. Just keep the control box on the shaft out of the water.

How deep does it detect?+

It reliably detects coin-sized targets down to roughly 8 inches, and larger objects deeper. The on-screen depth indicator tells you how far down a target is before you dig.

Is it a good gift, or good for kids?+

It's one of the most popular gifts in the hobby. The adjustable shaft fits kids and adults, it's lightweight, and it ships as a complete kit — ready to hunt out of the box.
